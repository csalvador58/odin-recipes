Instant Pot Kalua Pig

Description

Traditional Hawaiian kalua pig plate lunch cooked in a pressure cooker. Enjoy with cabbage, rice, and lomi salmon

Ingredients

  • 3 bacon slices
  • 5 pounds bone-in pork shoulder roast
  • 1½ tablespoons Red Hawaiian Coarse Sea Salt or ~1 tablespoon Red Hawaiian fine sea salt
  • 5 peeled garlic cloves
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cabbage cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Press the “Sauté" button and add bacon to your Instant Pot. Brown bacon on both sides.
  2. Slice pork into 2-3 pieces and create slits in pork to add a piece of garlic clove.
  3. Use 3/4 teaspoon of medium-coarse salt for every 1 pound of meat. Sprinkle evenly over the pork.
  4. Add pork into instant pot placed evenly on bacon slices.
  5. Pour in a cup of water then cover and lock the lid.
  6. Set instant pot to cook for 90 minutes under high pressure.
  7. Once the instant pot is done cooking and has depressurized
  8. Transfer the cooked pork to a large bowl and adjust the seasoning with water or salt if needed.
  9. Add cabbage into instant pot and replace the lid and cook the cabbage under high pressure for 1-5 minutes.
  10. Shred the pork, mix in cabbage, and serve over rice.
