Shoyu Chicken

Description

Savory and sweet chicken thighs slowly simmered in a garlic ginger shoyu sauce. Enjoy over rice and a side of mac salad.

Ingredients

  • 6 bone-in chicken thighs w/skin on
  • 1 cup water
  • 2/3 cup shoyu
  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • Green onions
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced or grated
  • 3-inch piece ginger, grated
  • Cornstarch slurry, to thicken sauce

Steps

  1. Create the braising liquid by mixing the water, shoyu, brown sugar, green onions, garlic & ginger.
  2. Refrigerate braising liquid up to 48 hours.
  3. Place chicken in a pot and evenly layered.
  4. Pour braising liquid into pot and bring to a boil.
  5. Once boiling, reduce to low. Cover pot and simmer for 30-35 mins.
  6. Optional: Broil chicken thighs in oven for a crispy and caramelized skin.
  7. Serve shoyu chicken over rice with a side of mac salad.
